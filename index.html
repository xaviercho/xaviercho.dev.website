COLLISION DETECTION

Technologies: C++, OpenGL

3D version of the Brownian motion problem, where a single particle that starts at a random position inside a volume bounded by an interactive polyhedron. The particle continues to move until it exits the volume. When the particle exits polygon through which it exits changes color to solid color and becomes opaque.
